Welcome to Hegel's Chapel

A weblog by Coleman "Little Dove" Gariety.

Hegel’s Chapel faithfully and lovingly continues the Hegelian project of demonstrating that the activity of spirit, the movement of the idea through history, can be known and understood by reason. We cast the light of reason on the respiration of history, causing it to shine.
